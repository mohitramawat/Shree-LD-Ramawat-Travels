Our Legacy

Founded By Visionary Leader

Late. Lal Das Ramawat

Founder & Visionary (1962)

Late. Lal Das Ramawat established Shree LD.Ramawat Travels in 1962 with a vision to revolutionize the travel industry in India. His dedication to providing safe, comfortable, and affordable transportation solutions laid the foundation for what has become one of India's most trusted travel service providers.

His core values of integrity, customer satisfaction, and innovation continue to guide our operations today. Under his leadership, the company grew from a small local service to a pan-India travel solution provider, touching the lives of millions of travelers.

"Travel is not just about reaching a destination, it's about the journey and the trust people place in us to get them there safely."

- Late. Lal Das Ramawat
